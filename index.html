About

The cuisine of Mexico has a great diversity of typical dishes, which is why it was recognized, by UNESCO, as Intangible Heritage of Humanity. The basic and representative ingredients of Mexican dishes are: corn, cilantro, chili, beans, piloncillo, nopal and tomato.
